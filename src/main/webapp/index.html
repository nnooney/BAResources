What We Do

Be Aware Resources provides a collection of resources ranging from information about academic requirements and health services to campus wide forum and event calendar. We partner with every institution to create a customized page that reflects everything you need to succeed.

Who We Are

Be Aware Resources was created by students for students to aggregate all resources necessary to succeed at each individual college campus on one platform. Student's can finally feel a sense of organization and guidance when utilizing our extensive and up-to-date database.
